How it's done

I'll usualy start out with making some sketches on paper that I then scan or photograph. I open it up in Photoshop, plug in my drawing tablet and start looking for reference images online, which I use to create a nice colour palette.

On a new layer - as you would on a new git branch - you can then start painting! Very rough at first, reworking the sketch and blocking in the colours. With light and dark colours you mix in a nice shading, giving volume to your figures. You keep adjusting your brush settings, trying out different brushes, while you work out all the details.
